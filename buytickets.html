How To Buy Tickets?

Secure a seat for your booty – actual or virtual— by doing the following:

  1. Choose a date & time on the calendar.
  2. Choose In-person or Online.
  3. Click the “Buy Tickets” button and follow the prompts on the purchase platform.

What Happens Next?

When you’re all done with that, you will receive an email with your e-ticket(s), as well as a link to the show’s website where you’ll be able to:

  • take a compatibility quiz before the show for a chance to be live-matched, if you went the in-person route
  • watch a livestream version of the play, if you went the online route.
  • comment on the show and vote to decide who advances, once it starts.

And won’t that be fun AF? It sure will.
